About Kinnaur

Kinnaur, a stunning region located in Himachal Pradesh, is known for its unique blend of lush valleys, high mountain ranges, and apple orchards. The region is famous for its Buddhist monasteries, quaint villages, and untouched natural beauty.

Culture

The culture of Kinnaur is deeply rooted in Tibetan traditions, with a strong influence of Buddhism. The region is known for its religious festivals like the Kinnaur Kailash Yatra and vibrant local arts such as Thangka painting and woodwork.

Food

The food in Kinnaur is heavily influenced by Tibetan cuisine, with dishes such as Thukpa (noodle soup), Momo (dumplings), and Chha Gosht (a meat curry) being popular. The cuisine is simple yet rich in flavors.
