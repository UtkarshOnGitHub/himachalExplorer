About Lahaul Spiti

Lahaul Spiti, a stunning and remote region in Himachal Pradesh, is known for its rugged terrain, high-altitude villages, and mesmerizing monasteries. The landscape here is an amalgamation of Tibetan culture, Buddhist heritage, and stunning natural beauty.

Culture

The culture of Lahaul Spiti is deeply influenced by Tibetan Buddhism. The region is home to many Buddhist monasteries, including the famous Key Monastery and Tabo Monastery. The local culture is reflected in the region's festivals, such as the Spiti Winter Festival, and the unique architecture of the region.

Food

The cuisine of Lahaul Spiti is influenced by Tibetan food and includes dishes like Thukpa, Chhang (fermented barley drink), and Yak meat. The food is hearty and ideal for the cold, high-altitude climate.
